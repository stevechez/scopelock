## Introduction

As a contractor or tradesman using BuildRail, efficiently managing your team and subcontractors is crucial. This guide walks you through the process of inviting team members and subcontractors to your BuildRail dashboard and setting appropriate permission levels, ensuring streamlined operations and secure access.

## Inviting Team Members and Subcontractors

### Step 1: Access the Team Management Section

1. **Log in to your BuildRail account.**
2. **Navigate to the dashboard's main menu.**
3. **Select "Team Management" from the options.**

### Step 2: Adding New Members

1. **Click on "Add New Member"** in the Team Management section.
2. **Enter the email address** of the team member or subcontractor you wish to invite.
3. **Select the role** from the dropdown menu:
   - **Admin**
   - **Project Manager**
   - **Tradesman**
   - **Subcontractor**

4. **Click "Send Invitation"** to dispatch an invitation email.

> **Pro Tip:** Ensure that email addresses are correct to avoid delays in onboarding.

## Setting Permission Levels

### Understanding Permission Roles

- **Admin:** Full access to all features, including user management and financials.
- **Project Manager:** Access to project-specific details and team communications.
- **Tradesman:** Access to assigned tasks and communication tools.
- **Subcontractor:** Limited access focused on task assignments and basic communication.

### Step 3: Configure Permissions

1. **Once the invitee accepts the invitation**, return to the Team Management section.
2. **Select the user** whose permissions you wish to configure.
3. **Adjust their permissions** by selecting or deselecting specific access rights such as:
   - **Viewing Bids**
   - **Managing Change Orders (ScopeLock)**
   - **Accessing Financials (PayRail)**
   - **Communication Tools (Comm Vault)**

4. **Click "Save"** to apply the changes.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly review and update permissions as team roles and project requirements evolve to maintain security and efficiency.

## Conclusion

By following these steps, you can effectively invite and manage your team members and subcontractors within BuildRail. Setting the right permission levels is key to ensuring that everyone has the necessary access to perform their roles efficiently while maintaining control over sensitive information.
