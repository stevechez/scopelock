## Introduction

Winning bids is crucial for your business. By saving your most successful bids as templates, you can quickly create future estimates in under 60 seconds, freeing up time to focus on what really matters: your projects. This guide will walk you through the steps to save and use bid templates effectively in BuildRail's Site Engine.

## Why Use Bid Templates?

Using bid templates allows you to:
- **Reduce Time**: Quickly generate estimates without starting from scratch.
- **Maintain Consistency**: Ensure every bid has the same professional quality.
- **Improve Accuracy**: Use tried-and-tested formats and content.

## Step-by-Step Guide to Saving Bids as Templates

### Step 1: Access Your Bid History

1. **Log in to BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access your BuildRail account.
2. **Navigate to Site Engine**: From the dashboard, click on **Site Engine**.
3. **View Bid History**: Click on **Bid History** to see all your previous bids.

### Step 2: Select a Successful Bid

1. **Identify a Successful Bid**: Review your bid history and select a bid that resulted in a winning contract.
2. **Open the Bid**: Click on the bid title to open the full bid details.

### Step 3: Save as a Template

1. **Save as Template**: With the bid open, click on the **Save as Template** button located at the top-right corner of the bid details page.
2. **Name Your Template**: Enter a descriptive name for your template to make it easily identifiable.
3. **Customize Template Settings**: Adjust any settings if necessary, such as pricing or specifications, to suit future projects.

### Step 4: Use Your Template for New Estimates

1. **Create a New Estimate**: Go back to the **Site Engine** and click on **Create Estimate**.
2. **Select Template**: Choose **Use Template** and select your newly saved template from the list.
3. **Customize the Estimate**: Make any project-specific adjustments to the estimate.
4. **Review and Send**: Double-check all details and send the estimate to your client.

> **Pro Tip**: Regularly update your templates with the latest pricing and materials to ensure your estimates are always accurate.

## Conclusion

By saving your successful bids as templates, you can streamline your estimating process significantly. This efficiency not only saves you time but also helps maintain a consistent level of quality across all your bids. Use this guide to optimize your workflow and focus more on delivering exceptional service to your clients.
