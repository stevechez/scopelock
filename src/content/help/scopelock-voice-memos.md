## Introduction

When you're on a noisy job site and need to issue a change order but don't have the time or peace to type, BuildRail's ScopeLock feature lets you attach a voice note. This guide will walk you through the process step-by-step, ensuring you can efficiently communicate changes without disrupting your workflow.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access ScopeLock

1. **Open BuildRail** on your mobile device.
2. Navigate to the **ScopeLock** section from your dashboard.

### Step 2: Create a New Change Order

1. Click on **New Change Order**.
2. Fill out any necessary fields that you can manage without typing much, such as selecting the relevant project or client from dropdown menus.

### Step 3: Record Your Voice Note

1. In the change order form, look for the **Attach File** option.
2. Select **Voice Note** from the available options.
3. Tap on the **Record** button.
4. Clearly state the details of the change order, including:
   - What is being changed
   - Reasons for the change
   - Any other relevant details
   
   > **Pro Tip:** Speak slowly and clearly to ensure the recording is easy to understand.

5. Tap **Stop** to end the recording.

### Step 4: Attach the Voice Note

1. Review the recorded voice note for accuracy.
2. Click **Attach** to add it to the change order.
3. **Save** the change order to ensure your voice note and any entered data are stored.

### Step 5: Send the Change Order

1. Once saved, select **Send** to dispatch the change order to the relevant parties.
2. Confirm the recipients and delivery method (e.g., email, in-app notification).

> **Pro Tip:** Always double-check the voice note and recipients before sending to avoid miscommunication.

## Conclusion

Using voice notes is an effective way to handle change orders when typing isn't feasible. By following these steps, you can ensure that all necessary information is conveyed quickly and efficiently, keeping your projects on track even in the hustle and bustle of a job site.
