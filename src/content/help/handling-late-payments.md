## Introduction

Keeping track of overdue invoices can be a hassle and manually reminding clients can be awkward. PayRail's automated reminder feature helps you handle this smoothly, ensuring you get paid without uncomfortable conversations. This guide will walk you through setting up automatic reminders for overdue invoices using PayRail.

## Setting Up Automated Reminders

### Step 1: Access PayRail

- Log into your BuildRail account.
- Navigate to the **PayRail** section from your dashboard.

### Step 2: Configure Reminder Settings

- Click on **Settings** in the PayRail menu.
- Select **Invoice Reminders**.
- Enable the **Automatic Reminders** option.

### Step 3: Customize Reminder Frequency

- Choose how frequently reminders should be sent:
  - **7 days overdue**
  - **14 days overdue**
  - **30 days overdue**
- You can select multiple options based on your needs.

> **Pro Tip:** Choosing multiple reminder intervals increases the chance of getting paid faster.

### Step 4: Personalize Reminder Messages

- Click on **Edit Message** next to each interval.
- Craft a professional, straightforward reminder message.
  - Example: "Dear [Client Name], this is a reminder that invoice #[Invoice Number] is currently [X days] overdue. Please make your payment at your earliest convenience. Thank you."

### Step 5: Review and Save

- Review your settings to ensure accuracy.
- Click **Save Changes** to apply your settings.

## Monitoring Reminders

### Step 6: Track Reminder Status

- Go back to the **Dashboard**.
- Under **Invoices**, you can view the status of reminders sent for each overdue invoice.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly check your dashboard to stay updated on which invoices are still unpaid.

## Conclusion

By following these steps, you can streamline your invoicing process, minimize awkward interactions, and ensure timely payments. Automating reminders with PayRail not only saves you time but also helps maintain positive client relationships.
