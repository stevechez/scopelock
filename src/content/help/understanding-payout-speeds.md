## Introduction

As a contractor using BuildRail's PayRail feature for client payments, understanding when you'll receive your funds is crucial for managing cash flow. Stripe, our payment processor, handles these transactions. This article provides a detailed explanation of Stripe processing times and when you can expect the funds in your checking account.

## How Stripe Processing Works

Stripe processes payments in a few distinct stages. Here's how it typically works:

1. **Authorization**: When a payment is initiated, Stripe authorizes it, ensuring the client's bank can cover the charge.
2. **Capture**: Once authorized, the payment is captured. This means the money is deducted from the client's account.
3. **Payout**: Funds are then transferred from Stripe to your bank account.

## Standard Processing Times

- **Initial Transactions**: For new users, the first payout may take up to 7 days due to account verification processes.
- **Regular Transactions**: Once your account is established, payouts typically occur on a rolling 2-day schedule. This means funds from transactions processed today will usually be deposited in your account in two business days.
- **Weekends and Holidays**: Payouts are only made on business days. Transactions processed on weekends or holidays will be delayed until the next business day.

## Factors Affecting Processing Times

Several factors can influence the processing time:

- **Bank Delays**: Some banks may take longer to process incoming funds, which can extend the time it takes for money to appear in your account.
- **Account Verification**: New accounts or those undergoing changes may experience delayed payouts as Stripe verifies the information.
- **Risk Evaluation**: Certain transactions may be subject to additional review, affecting payout times.

## Viewing Your Payout Schedule

To view your payout schedule in Stripe:

1. **Log in to BuildRail** and navigate to the **PayRail** section.
2. Click on **View Payouts** to see upcoming and past payouts.
3. Check the **Expected Arrival Date** for each transaction.

## Pro Tips

> **Pro Tip**: To optimize cash flow, plan your projects around the payout schedule. Knowing when funds will clear can help you manage expenses and payroll effectively.

> **Pro Tip**: Ensure your bank details are accurate and up-to-date in BuildRail to avoid any payout delays.

## Conclusion

Understanding Stripe’s processing times ensures you can accurately plan your finances and avoid cash flow surprises. Regularly review your payout schedule and stay informed about any changes in Stripe’s processing policies. For further assistance, contact BuildRail support or consult Stripe's documentation for more details.
