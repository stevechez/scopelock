## Introduction

Losing access to your phone can prevent you from logging into your BuildRail account, disrupting your work. This guide provides a clear path to regain access and get back to managing your projects efficiently.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. **Access BuildRail on a Desktop or Laptop**

- **Navigate to the BuildRail Login Page:** Use a web browser on your computer.
- **Enter Your Email and Password:** Type the credentials you usually use to log into your BuildRail account.

### 2. **Select 'Forgot Your Password?'**

- **Click on 'Forgot Your Password?'** This can be found beneath the password entry field.
- **Follow the Prompts:** You will be guided through the process to reset your password using your registered email address.

### 3. **Contact BuildRail Support**

If you are unable to reset your password or need further assistance:

- **Email Support:** Send an email to support@buildrail.com with the subject line "Lost Phone - Account Access Help".
- **Include the Following Information:**
  - Your full name
  - Registered email address
  - A brief description of your issue

### 4. **Verify Your Identity**

- **Wait for a Response:** Our team will contact you within 24 hours.
- **Provide Any Requested Information:** You may be asked to verify your identity by answering security questions or providing additional details about your account.

### 5. **Set Up New Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**

Once access is restored:

- **Navigate to Account Settings:** After logging in, go to your account settings.
- **Select 'Security':** Here, you can set up a new two-factor authentication method.
- **Choose an Alternative Method:** Consider using a backup phone number or an authenticator app to prevent future access issues.

> **Pro Tip:** Always keep a backup method for two-factor authentication to avoid being locked out of your account.

## Conclusion

Losing access to your phone shouldn't halt your work. By following these steps, you can regain access to your BuildRail account swiftly. For further assistance, don’t hesitate to contact our support team. Your time is valuable, and we’re here to ensure you get back to managing your projects with minimal downtime.
