## Introduction

Launching your local service website with BuildRail is a straightforward process designed to establish your online presence quickly. Follow these steps to fill out your company profile and go live in just 10 minutes.

## Step 1: Sign In to BuildRail

1. **Log In**: Access your BuildRail account using your credentials.
2. **Navigate to Dashboard**: Once logged in, you'll be directed to your main dashboard.

## Step 2: Access the Company Profile Section

1. **Locate Profile Settings**: On your dashboard, find the menu on the left-hand side.
2. **Select Company Profile**: Click on "Company Profile" to begin editing.

## Step 3: Fill Out Your Company Profile

### Basic Information

- **Business Name**: Enter your registered business name.
- **Contact Information**: Add your business phone number and email.
- **Address**: Provide your business address to ensure local search optimization.

### Professional Details

- **Services Offered**: Clearly define the services you provide. Use bullets for clarity.
- **Service Areas**: List the locations you serve to attract local customers.

### Visual Elements

- **Upload Logo**: Add a professional logo for brand identity.
- **Portfolio Images**: Showcase your work with high-quality images.

> **Pro Tip**: Use a high-resolution logo and recent images to make a strong first impression.

## Step 4: Customize Your Website

1. **Choose a Template**: Select from BuildRail's pre-designed templates that best fit your business style.
2. **Edit Content**: Modify the text on your homepage to reflect your unique selling points and mission statement.

## Step 5: Preview and Launch

1. **Preview Website**: Use the Preview function to see how your site appears to potential clients.
2. **Go Live**: Satisfied with your setup? Hit the "Publish" button to make your site live.

> **Pro Tip**: Regularly update your website with new projects and testimonials to maintain engagement and attract new clients.

## Conclusion

By following these steps, you'll have a fully functional local service website ready to attract and convert leads. Taking just 10 minutes to set this up will significantly enhance your business's online presence and accessibility.
