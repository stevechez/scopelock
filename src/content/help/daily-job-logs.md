## Introduction

In the construction industry, keeping clients informed is crucial. With BuildRail's smCrewLens, your crew can efficiently submit end-of-day progress photos and notes, ensuring transparency and satisfaction. This guide will walk you through the process.

## Getting Started

To make use of smCrewLens, ensure that each crew member has access to the BuildRail app and their user roles permit them to upload media and notes.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Access smCrewLens

- **Log in to BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access the BuildRail app on your mobile device.
- **Navigate to smCrewLens**: From the main dashboard, select the smCrewLens feature.

### 2. Capture Progress Photos

- **Select 'Capture New'**: Tap on the camera icon in smCrewLens to start capturing photos.
- **Take Photos**: Ensure photos are clear and cover all significant areas of progress.
- **Review and Save**: After taking each photo, review them for clarity and relevance, then save them to the day's log.

> **Pro Tip**: Capture photos from multiple angles to provide a comprehensive view of the progress.

### 3. Add Notes

- **Select 'Add Note'**: Once photos are saved, tap on the note icon to add detailed descriptions.
- **Write Detailed Descriptions**: Mention specific tasks completed, any issues encountered, and work planned for the next day.
- **Link Notes to Photos**: Ensure each note corresponds with the relevant photo by tagging them appropriately.

### 4. Submit to Client

- **Review Entries**: Before submission, review all photos and notes for accuracy and completeness.
- **Submit**: Tap 'Submit to Client' to send the report directly to the client’s dashboard.

> **Pro Tip**: Regular submissions not only keep clients informed but also build trust and transparency.

## Conclusion

By following these steps, your crew can efficiently use smCrewLens to submit end-of-day progress updates. This process enhances communication with clients and ensures they are always in the loop regarding project developments.

Make smCrewLens a part of your daily routine for streamlined project management and improved client satisfaction.
