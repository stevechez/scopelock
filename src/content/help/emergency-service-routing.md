## Introduction

Emergencies can strike at any time, and timely communication is crucial for addressing urgent leak or repair calls. With BuildRail's Comm Vault, you can set up "High Priority" SMS alerts to ensure that critical situations are addressed swiftly. Follow the steps below to configure your alerts and stay on top of emergencies.

## Setting Up 'High Priority' SMS Alerts

### Step 1: Access Comm Vault

1. **Log in to BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access your BuildRail account.
2. **Navigate to Comm Vault**: From the dashboard, locate and select the **Comm Vault** module.

### Step 2: Create a New Alert

1. **Select 'Alerts'**: In the Comm Vault interface, find and click on the **'Alerts'** tab.
2. **Click 'New Alert'**: Initiate the creation of a new alert by clicking the **'New Alert'** button.

### Step 3: Configure Alert Details

1. **Name Your Alert**: Enter a descriptive name for your alert, such as "Emergency Leak Alert".
2. **Priority Level**: Set the priority to **'High Priority'** to ensure these alerts are treated with urgency.
3. **Alert Type**: Choose **'SMS'** as the method of communication.

### Step 4: Specify Alert Criteria

1. **Trigger Condition**: Define the conditions that trigger the alert. For example, select "Leak" or "Emergency Repair" as the trigger events.
2. **Recipient List**: Add the phone numbers of all relevant team members who need to receive this alert.

### Step 5: Customize Message Content

1. **Compose Message**: Write a concise, clear message that specifies the nature of the emergency and any immediate actions required.
   - Example: "Urgent: Leak detected at [Location]. Immediate assistance needed. Contact [Name] at [Phone Number]."

### Step 6: Review and Save

1. **Review Configuration**: Double-check all details to ensure accuracy.
2. **Save Alert**: Click **'Save'** to activate your high-priority SMS alert.

> **Pro Tip**: Regularly update your recipient list to ensure all key personnel receive alerts. Test your alerts periodically to verify functionality.

## Conclusion

By setting up "High Priority" SMS alerts in BuildRail, you're equipping your team to respond swiftly to emergencies, minimizing damage and downtime. For further assistance, consult the BuildRail support team or visit our Help Center.
