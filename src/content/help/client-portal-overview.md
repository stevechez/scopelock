## Introduction

When you provide your clients with a tracking link to view their project status, it's crucial to understand what they see. This ensures you can guide them effectively and address any questions or concerns. This article will walk you through the client's experience step-by-step.

## Step-by-Step Client Experience

### 1. Opening the Tracking Link

- **Client Action**: Click on the provided tracking link.
- **What They See**: A welcome screen with your company logo and a brief greeting message.

### 2. Viewing Project Dashboard

- **Client Action**: After the welcome message, they are directed to the main project dashboard.
- **What They See**:
  - **Project Overview**: High-level summary including project name, start date, expected completion date, and current status.
  - **Visual Progress Bar**: A graphical representation of how far along the project is, percentage-wise.

### 3. Detailed Project Breakdown

- **Client Action**: Click on "View Details" for a more in-depth look.
- **What They See**:
  - **Task List**: A detailed list of tasks with statuses (e.g., Pending, In Progress, Completed).
  - **Milestones**: Major project milestones and their completion status.
  - **Comments Section**: A log of all communications related to each task, ensuring transparency.

### 4. Change Orders

- **Client Action**: Navigate to the "Change Orders" tab.
- **What They See**:
  - **Pending Approvals**: Any change orders that require their review and approval.
  - **Approved Changes**: A list of all approved change orders with details on cost and timeline adjustments.
  - **Declined Requests**: Any change orders they have declined, with notes for reference.

### 5. Payment Information

- **Client Action**: Access the "Payments" section.
- **What They See**:
  - **Invoice Summary**: A list of all invoices, including paid, pending, and upcoming.
  - **Payment History**: Detailed breakdown of all payments received and their respective dates.
  - **Payment Options**: Instructions on how to make a payment through PayRail, ensuring fast and secure transactions.

> **Pro Tip**: Encourage clients to use the "Comments Section" for any questions or clarifications. This keeps all communication in one place and helps avoid miscommunications.

## Conclusion

Understanding what your client sees when they click the tracking link helps you provide better support and ensures a smooth project experience. By guiding them through the dashboard and addressing their concerns promptly, you foster trust and transparency. Always encourage your clients to reach out if they have any questions about what they see on their dashboard.
