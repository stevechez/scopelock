## Introduction

Efficient communication is crucial in managing construction projects. BuildRail offers customizable SMS and email alerts for you, your office manager, and your foremen. This guide will walk you through the process of setting up these alerts to ensure the right information reaches the right people at the right time.

## Accessing Alert Settings

To customize your alert preferences in BuildRail, follow these steps:

1. **Log in to BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access your BuildRail account.
2. **Navigate to Settings**: Click on your profile icon in the upper right corner and select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. **Select Notifications**: In the Settings menu, click on **Notifications**. This section allows you to manage all your alert preferences.

## Customizing SMS and Email Alerts

### Step 1: Choose Alert Type

- **SMS Alerts**: Enable or disable SMS alerts for immediate, on-the-go updates.
- **Email Alerts**: Opt for email alerts for detailed information and records.

### Step 2: Define Alert Recipients

1. **For You**: 
   - Click on **My Alerts**.
   - Toggle the switch for each alert type you wish to receive. Options include project updates, bid wins, and payment confirmations.

2. **For Your Office Manager**:
   - Click on **Office Manager Alerts**.
   - Enter the office manager's contact details.
   - Select which alerts they should receive. Typically, choose administrative notifications such as payment collections.

3. **For Your Foremen**:
   - Click on **Foremen Alerts**.
   - Add each foreman's contact information.
   - Assign alerts relevant to their tasks, like scope changes and communication updates.

### Step 3: Set Alert Frequency

- **Instant**: Receive alerts as they happen.
- **Daily Summary**: Get a compiled report of alerts at the end of each day.
- **Weekly Summary**: Opt for a weekly digest every Friday.

**Pro Tip**: 
> **Customize Alert Levels**: Tailor the urgency of alerts by setting high-priority notifications for critical issues only. This helps reduce distractions and keeps your team focused on what matters most.

## Saving and Testing Your Settings

1. **Save Changes**: After setting up your preferences, click **Save** to apply the changes.
2. **Test Alerts**: Send a test alert to ensure that all recipients receive the notifications as intended.

## Conclusion

Customizing alerts in BuildRail enhances team communication and project efficiency. By following these steps, you ensure that crucial information reaches the right people promptly, allowing for timely decisions and actions.
