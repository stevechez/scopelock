## Introduction

Referral discounts are an excellent way to incentivize past clients to refer new business to you. In Comm Vault, setting up a referral discount is straightforward. This guide will walk you through the process so you can start rewarding your clients and growing your business.

## Steps to Create a Referral Discount

### Step 1: Access Comm Vault

1. **Log In**: Start by logging into your BuildRail account.
2. **Navigate to Comm Vault**: On the dashboard, click on **Comm Vault** in the main navigation bar.

### Step 2: Set Up the Referral Discount

1. **Go to Discounts**:
   - Within Comm Vault, locate the **Discounts** section in the sidebar.
2. **Create New Discount**:
   - Click on the **Create New Discount** button.
3. **Select Referral Discount**:
   - In the discount type options, select **Referral Discount**.

### Step 3: Define the Discount Parameters

1. **Discount Amount**:
   - Enter the amount or percentage you wish to offer as a referral discount.
2. **Eligibility Criteria**:
   - Define who qualifies for this discount. Typically, this might include clients who refer new customers that result in successful projects.
3. **Validity Period**:
   - Set the duration for which the referral discount will be valid.
4. **Terms and Conditions**:
   - Clearly outline any terms and conditions related to the discount.

### Step 4: Customize the Referral Message

1. **Craft Your Message**:
   - Write a personalized message that will be sent to your clients, informing them about the referral program. Make it clear, concise, and motivating.
2. **Preview**:
   - Use the preview function to ensure your message looks professional and engaging.

### Step 5: Activate and Monitor

1. **Activate Discount**:
   - Once satisfied with the setup, click **Activate** to make the referral discount live.
2. **Monitor Performance**:
   - Regularly check the performance of your referral discount in the **Reports** section of Comm Vault to see how effectively it is bringing in new business.

## Pro Tips

> **Pro Tip**: Encourage clients to share their referral links through multiple channels, such as email, social media, and word-of-mouth, to maximize reach.

> **Pro Tip**: Use testimonials from satisfied clients to enhance trust and encourage new referrals.

## Conclusion

By setting up a referral discount in Comm Vault, you create a win-win situation for both you and your clients. This simple yet effective strategy can significantly boost your client base and enhance loyalty. Start today and see the positive impact on your business.
