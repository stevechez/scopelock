## Introduction
ScopeLock is your tool to ensure every change in project scope is documented and billed accurately. Whether you're adding tasks, materials, or adjusting timelines, ScopeLock keeps your work protected and paid. This guide walks you through generating a change order directly from your phone while at the job site.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access BuildRail on Your Mobile Device
- **Open the BuildRail app** on your smartphone. Ensure you're logged in with your contractor credentials.

### Step 2: Navigate to the Project
- From the **dashboard**, tap on **Projects**.
- Find and select the project you are currently working on. Use the search bar for quick access if necessary.

### Step 3: Initiate a Change Order
- Inside the project, navigate to the **ScopeLock** section.
- Tap on **Create Change Order**. This will open a new change order form.

### Step 4: Fill Out Change Order Details
- **Title**: Input a concise title for the change order.
- **Description**: Provide a detailed description of the changes required. Be specific about tasks, materials, and timelines.
- **Cost Estimate**: Enter an estimated cost for the additional work. Make sure to include labor, materials, and any other expenses.

> **Pro Tip:** Always take photos of the site conditions to document the need for changes. Attach these directly in the form using the camera icon.

### Step 5: Review and Confirm
- Double-check all entered details for accuracy.
- Tap **Review** to see a summary of the change order.
- Make any necessary adjustments before proceeding.

### Step 6: Submit the Change Order
- Once satisfied, tap **Submit** to send the change order for approval.
- You will receive a notification once the change order is approved or if further information is needed.

## Conclusion
Generating a ScopeLock change order from your phone is straightforward and ensures you're compensated for every adjustment. Keep your projects on track and your cash flow steady by documenting changes immediately as they occur.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly update your BuildRail app to access the latest features and improvements.

By following these steps, you maintain clear communication with your clients and safeguard your business against unpaid work. Stay proactive and professional with ScopeLock at your fingertips.
