## Introduction
Synchronizing your BuildRail invoices and expenses with QuickBooks Online helps you streamline your financial tracking and reporting. This guide provides clear instructions on how to connect BuildRail to QuickBooks Online and automate the transfer of financial data.

## Prerequisites
Before you begin, ensure you have:
- An active BuildRail account.
- A QuickBooks Online account with administrative access.
- Internet access for both platforms.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access BuildRail Settings
1. Log in to your BuildRail account.
2. Go to the **Settings** menu, located in the top-right corner of the dashboard.

### Step 2: Connect to QuickBooks Online
1. Within the **Settings** menu, find and select **Integrations**.
2. Click on **Connect to QuickBooks Online**.
3. You will be redirected to QuickBooks Online. Sign in with your QuickBooks credentials.

### Step 3: Authorize the Connection
1. Once logged in, you will see a request to authorize BuildRail to connect with your QuickBooks Online account.
2. Review the permissions and click **Authorize** to grant access.

> **Pro Tip:** Ensure you have the necessary permissions in QuickBooks to authorize third-party applications. Contact your QuickBooks admin if needed.

### Step 4: Configure Sync Settings
1. Return to the BuildRail **Settings** page.
2. Under **QuickBooks Online Integration**, configure your sync preferences:
   - **Invoices**: Select whether to sync all invoices or specific ones.
   - **Expenses**: Choose to sync all expenses or filter by category or date.
3. Save your settings.

### Step 5: Initiate Data Sync
1. Navigate to the **Invoice** or **Expense** section of BuildRail.
2. Select the items you wish to sync.
3. Click on **Sync to QuickBooks Online**.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly schedule syncs to ensure your financial data is always up-to-date in QuickBooks Online.

## Troubleshooting Tips
- If the sync fails, verify your internet connection and ensure that both BuildRail and QuickBooks Online services are operational.
- Recheck your QuickBooks permissions if authorization is unsuccessful.
- Contact BuildRail Support if you encounter persistent issues.

## Conclusion
By connecting BuildRail to QuickBooks Online, you streamline your financial processes, reduce manual data entry, and enhance the accuracy of your financial reporting. Follow these steps regularly to keep your financial data synchronized and up-to-date.
