## Introduction

As a contractor, you often find yourself on job sites with spotty cell service or no Wi-Fi. Fortunately, the BuildRail mobile app is designed to function even in these challenging conditions. Follow these tips to ensure seamless operation and avoid disruptions to your workflow.

## Preparation Steps

### 1. Offline Availability

Before heading to the job site, make sure your necessary data is available offline:

- **Sync Data:** Open the BuildRail app while you have a strong internet connection. Navigate to each section you anticipate needing (e.g., Site Engine, Comm Vault, etc.) and allow the app to sync fully.
- **Download Important Documents:** Save any essential documents or communications directly to your device. This ensures that you can access them without needing to connect to the internet.

### 2. Optimize App Settings

Adjust your app settings to improve performance:

- **Enable Offline Mode:** Go to the app settings and switch on the "Offline Mode" option. This mode optimizes the app's functionality for environments with limited connectivity.
- **Limit Background Data:** Restrict background data usage to conserve battery and reduce unnecessary data requests.

## On-Site Usage

### 3. Accessing Offline Data

While on-site, you can still access important information:

- **Check Synced Content:** Navigate to the sections you synced previously. You can view all information that was downloaded while you were connected.
- **Modify Offline:** Make changes or updates to documents or tasks. These will be saved locally and automatically synced when you regain connectivity.

### 4. Manage Communications

Even with limited service, communication remains key:

- **Draft Messages:** In Comm Vault, draft any messages or emails you need to send. These drafts will be sent automatically once you regain service.
- **Record Voice Notes:** Use the voice note feature to capture important details or instructions. These can be shared later when connectivity is restored.

## Post-Job Site Actions

### 5. Sync Updates

Once you're back in an area with good connectivity:

- **Manual Sync:** Open the BuildRail app and navigate to each section to trigger a manual sync. This ensures all your offline work is uploaded to the cloud.
- **Verify Data Integrity:** Double-check that all changes and messages have been updated and sent as intended.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly update the BuildRail app to the latest version to benefit from performance improvements and offline functionality enhancements.

## Conclusion

By following these steps, you can effectively use the BuildRail mobile app even in areas with low cell service or no Wi-Fi. Preparing your device before heading to a site and knowing how to manage offline data are critical in maintaining productivity and communication. Stay ahead of the game, even when connectivity is not on your side.
