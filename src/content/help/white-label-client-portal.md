## Introduction

Customizing the portal your clients see is an excellent way to reinforce your brand identity. With BuildRail, you can easily add your own logo and brand colors. This guide will walk you through the steps to personalize your client portal.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access the Branding Settings

1. **Log into BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access your BuildRail account.
2. **Navigate to Settings**: Click on the user icon in the top right corner and select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. **Choose Branding**: In the settings menu, locate and click on **Branding**. This will take you to the customization options for your client portal.

### Step 2: Upload Your Logo

1. **Select the Logo Upload Section**: On the branding page, find the section labeled **Logo**.
2. **Upload Your Logo**: Click the **Upload** button. Browse your computer to select the logo file you wish to use. Make sure it meets the following requirements:
   - **File Format**: JPEG or PNG
   - **Size**: Maximum 2 MB
   - **Dimensions**: Recommended 300x100 pixels for optimal display
3. **Save Changes**: After uploading, click **Save Changes** to apply your logo to the client portal.

> **Pro Tip:** Use a high-quality, transparent PNG for a professional look.

### Step 3: Set Your Brand Colors

1. **Access the Color Settings**: Below the logo section, find **Brand Colors**.
2. **Choose Your Colors**: 
   - **Primary Color**: Click on the color box next to **Primary Color**. Use the color picker or enter a HEX code for your primary brand color.
   - **Secondary Color**: Repeat the process for the **Secondary Color**.
3. **Preview Changes**: A preview will show how your colors will appear in the portal.
4. **Save Changes**: Once satisfied with the color selection, click **Save Changes**.

> **Pro Tip:** Consistent use of brand colors enhances recognition and professionalism.

## Conclusion

By following these steps, you can effectively customize your BuildRail client portal to reflect your brand's identity. This not only enhances your professional appearance but also builds trust with your clients. For further customization options or assistance, consult the BuildRail Help Center or contact support.
