## Introduction

Managing new leads efficiently is crucial for maximizing your business opportunities. With BuildRail's Site Engine, you can easily view, filter, and respond to leads to ensure no potential job is missed. This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to handle new leads using Site Engine.

## Viewing New Leads

To access new leads from your Site Engine contact forms, follow these steps:

1. **Log in to BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access the BuildRail dashboard.
2. **Navigate to Site Engine**: From the main menu, select **Site Engine**.
3. **Access Leads**: Click on the **Leads** tab to view all incoming inquiries.

## Filtering Leads

Filtering helps you prioritize and manage your leads effectively. Here's how you can filter your leads:

1. **Open Filter Options**: On the Leads page, locate the **Filter** button at the top.
2. **Set Filter Criteria**: Choose from options such as:
   - **Date Received**: See leads from a specific timeframe.
   - **Project Type**: Focus on leads relevant to your expertise.
   - **Location**: Target specific geographic areas.
3. **Apply Filters**: Click **Apply** to view the filtered results.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly update your filter preferences to align with your business goals.

## Instantly Responding to Leads

Quick responses can significantly increase your chance of winning a job. Here's how to respond instantly:

1. **Select a Lead**: Click on a lead from your filtered list to view details.
2. **Compose a Response**: Use the **Quick Reply** feature to draft your message.
3. **Send the Response**: Hit **Send** to deliver your message instantly.

## Best Practices

- **Set Notifications**: Enable push or email notifications to stay updated on new leads as they come in.
- **Use Templates**: Save time by creating response templates for commonly asked questions.
- **Follow Up**: Schedule follow-ups using Comm Vault to maintain engagement with leads.

By following these steps, you can efficiently manage your Site Engine leads and increase your chances of securing more projects. For further assistance, visit our [Help Center](#) or contact support.
