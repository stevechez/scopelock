## Introduction

Offering flexible payment options can give you a competitive edge and help close more deals. BuildRail allows you to offer "Buy Now, Pay Later" options to your clients through our integrated financing partners. This guide provides a step-by-step process to set up and manage these options effectively.

## Step 1: Access Financing Options

1. **Log in to BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access the platform.
2. **Navigate to PayRail**: Once logged in, go to the PayRail section of your dashboard.
3. **Select Financing Options**: Click on the 'Financing Options' tab to view available partners and plans.

## Step 2: Choose a Financing Partner

1. **Review Partner Details**: Each partner will have different terms and conditions. Review the details carefully.
2. **Select a Partner**: Choose a partner that aligns with your business needs and your clients' preferences.

   > **Pro Tip**: Consider the interest rates, approval times, and customer service reputation when selecting a partner.

## Step 3: Set Up Financing Plans for Your Clients

1. **Create a Financing Profile**: Enter details about your typical project size, client base, and any specific requirements.
2. **Customize the Plan**: Adjust the terms, such as payment periods and interest rates, if customization is available.
3. **Save the Plan**: Save your settings to make the plan available for future client proposals.

## Step 4: Present Financing Options to Clients

1. **Include in Proposals**: When creating a bid using Site Engine, attach the financing options to the proposal.
2. **Discuss During Client Meetings**: Clearly explain the benefits and terms of the "Buy Now, Pay Later" options to your clients.

   > **Pro Tip**: Highlight how spreading the payments can help your clients manage their cash flow while getting the work done sooner.

## Step 5: Monitor and Manage Financing Agreements

1. **Track Client Approvals**: Use Comm Vault to track client communications and approvals regarding the financing plan.
2. **Manage Agreements**: Check the status of financing agreements through PayRail to ensure timely payments.

## Conclusion

By offering "Buy Now, Pay Later" options through BuildRail's integrated financing partners, you can provide flexibility to your clients and secure more projects. Follow these steps to set up and manage these options efficiently.
