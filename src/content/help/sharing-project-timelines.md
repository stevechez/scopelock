## Introduction

Providing clients with a live-updating Gantt chart for their kitchen remodel is crucial for transparency and managing expectations. With BuildRail, you can easily share a dynamic schedule that clients can access anytime. This guide walks you through the process of creating and sharing a live Gantt chart.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access the Project Dashboard

1. **Log in to BuildRail.**
2. Navigate to the **Projects** section from the main menu.
3. Select the relevant kitchen remodel project you want to share with your client.

### Step 2: Create or Update Your Gantt Chart

1. In the project dashboard, click on **Schedule**.
2. Use the Gantt chart tool to lay out the project timeline, including:
   - Start and end dates for each task.
   - Dependencies between tasks.
   - Milestones and critical deadlines.

   **Pro Tip:** Ensure all tasks are clearly labeled and accurately reflect the project scope. This clarity helps prevent misunderstandings.

### Step 3: Enable Live Updates

1. Once your Gantt chart is configured, locate the **Settings** gear icon.
2. Click **Enable Live Updates**. This feature ensures that any changes you make are automatically reflected in the client's view.

### Step 4: Share the Gantt Chart with Your Client

1. Return to the project dashboard and select **Share**.
2. Choose **Gantt Chart** from the sharing options.
3. Enter your client's email address.
4. Customize permissions:
   - **View-only:** Clients can view the chart but cannot make changes.
   - **Comment-enabled:** Clients can leave comments for clarification or questions.

   **Pro Tip:** Opt for **Comment-enabled** to facilitate easy communication and address any client concerns promptly.

5. Click **Send Invitation**. Your client will receive an email with a link to access the live-updating Gantt chart.

### Step 5: Confirm Receipt and Access

1. Follow up with your client to ensure they have received the invitation and can access the Gantt chart.
2. Encourage them to bookmark the link for easy reference throughout the project.

## Conclusion

By providing your client with a live-updating Gantt chart, you foster transparency and trust while keeping project timelines clear. BuildRail's intuitive platform makes this process straightforward, enhancing client satisfaction and project management efficiency.
