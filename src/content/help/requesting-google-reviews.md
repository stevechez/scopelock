## Introduction

BuildRail's PayRail feature streamlines your invoicing and payment collection. To maximize your reputation and visibility, you can automatically request Google reviews from clients the moment they pay their invoices. This guide will walk you through setting up this automation.

## Benefits

- **Increase your online presence**: More reviews lead to better visibility.
- **Boost client trust**: Positive reviews enhance credibility.
- **Reduce manual follow-ups**: Save time and effort by automating the review request process.

## Prerequisites

Before setting up this feature, ensure you have the following:

- A BuildRail account with access to the PayRail module.
- A Google My Business account set up for your contracting business.
- Administrative access to modify BuildRail settings.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Access PayRail Settings

1. **Log in to BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access your BuildRail dashboard.
2. **Navigate to PayRail**: From the main menu, select **PayRail**.
3. **Open Settings**: Click on the **Settings** gear icon within the PayRail module.

### Step 2: Integrate Google My Business

1. **Select Integrations**: In the PayRail settings, go to the **Integrations** tab.
2. **Connect Google My Business**:
   - Click on **Connect** under Google My Business.
   - Follow the prompts to authorize BuildRail to access your Google account.

### Step 3: Configure Google Review Request

1. **Enable Automatic Requests**: In the Google My Business integration settings, toggle the option to **Enable Automatic Review Requests**.
2. **Customize Message**:
   - Enter a personalized message that will be sent to your clients. For example:
     > "Thank you for your payment! We'd appreciate it if you could take a moment to leave us a review on Google."
3. **Set Trigger Condition**:
   - Ensure the trigger is set to activate when an invoice is marked as **Paid**.

### Step 4: Test the Setup

1. **Create a Test Invoice**: Generate a test invoice in PayRail and mark it as **Paid**.
2. **Verify Request**: Check your email or test account to ensure the Google Review request is sent correctly.

## Troubleshooting

- **Issue: No Review Request Sent**
  - Ensure your Google My Business account is correctly linked.
  - Double-check that the trigger condition is set to "Paid" invoices.

- **Issue: Incorrect Review Message**
  - Revisit the message customization section and save changes.

## Conclusion

By automating Google Review requests through PayRail, you can efficiently boost your business's online reputation. Follow these steps to set it up, and watch your visibility and client trust grow.

> **Pro Tip**: Regularly monitor your reviews and respond to them to maintain strong client relationships.
