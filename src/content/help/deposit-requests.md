## Introduction

In the construction business, ensuring you receive an upfront deposit before scheduling your crew is crucial for cash flow and project commitment. BuildRail's PayRail feature enables you to set up and automatically send deposit invoices to clients. This guide provides a step-by-step process to help you efficiently manage upfront payments.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access PayRail

1. **Log into BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access your BuildRail account.
2. **Navigate to PayRail**: From the dashboard, locate and click on the **PayRail** tab in the main navigation menu.

### Step 2: Configure Deposit Settings

1. **Select Project**: Choose the project for which you need to set up the deposit invoice.
2. **Access Deposit Settings**: Within the project dashboard, click on **Billing Options** and then select **Deposit Settings**.

### Step 3: Set Up Deposit Invoice

1. **Enable Deposit Requirement**: Toggle the **Require Deposit** option to 'On'.
2. **Specify Deposit Amount**: Enter the deposit amount or percentage required upfront. Ensure this aligns with your contractual agreement.
3. **Add Invoice Details**: Provide clear descriptions for the work covered by the deposit to avoid any client confusion.

### Step 4: Automate Invoice Sending

1. **Set Automation Preferences**: In the **Invoice Settings**, choose to **Automatically Send Invoice** upon project acceptance.
2. **Select Recipients**: Confirm the client’s contact information is correct for sending the invoice.
3. **Schedule Sending**: Opt to send the invoice immediately or set a specific date and time.

### Step 5: Review and Save

1. **Review Details**: Double-check all invoice details for accuracy.
2. **Save Settings**: Click **Save** to ensure all configurations are stored.

### Step 6: Notification and Tracking

1. **Enable Notifications**: Opt to receive notifications when the invoice is viewed and paid.
2. **Track Payments**: Use the **Transaction History** in PayRail to monitor payment status.

> **Pro Tip:** Always confirm receipt of deposit before scheduling your crew to avoid project delays.

## Conclusion

By setting up automated upfront deposit invoices in BuildRail, you streamline your payment process, secure your finances, and maintain optimal project scheduling. Ensure all your invoice settings are correctly configured, and make use of automation features to reduce manual tasks and enhance efficiency.
