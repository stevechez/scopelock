## Introduction

Connecting your business bank account to PayRail allows for seamless and secure transactions. We use Stripe Connect to ensure the highest level of security. Follow these steps to set up your account swiftly and securely.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

- Your business bank account details.
- Access to your BuildRail account.
- An internet connection.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access PayRail

1. **Log in to your BuildRail Account.**
   - Use your registered email and password.

2. **Navigate to PayRail.**
   - From the dashboard, locate and click on the **PayRail** module.

### Step 2: Initiate Connection with Stripe

1. **Select 'Connect Bank Account'.**
   - This option is found within the PayRail module.

2. **Choose 'Stripe Connect'.**
   - You will be redirected to the Stripe Connect page.

### Step 3: Connect Your Bank Account

1. **Enter Your Business Information.**
   - Fill in your business name, address, and other required details.

2. **Provide Your Bank Account Details.**
   - Ensure accuracy to prevent errors during transactions.

3. **Verify Your Identity.**
   - Follow the prompts to verify your identity. This may include uploading identification documents.

### Step 4: Authorize the Connection

1. **Review the Terms.**
   - Read through the Stripe terms and conditions carefully.

2. **Click 'Authorize'.**
   - By authorizing, you give Stripe permission to facilitate transactions for you.

### Step 5: Confirmation

- **Receive Confirmation.**
  - A confirmation screen will appear once your bank account is successfully linked.

- **Check Your Email.**
  - Stripe will send an email confirmation for your records.

## Troubleshooting

- **Error Messages:**
  - Double-check all entries for accuracy if you encounter errors.
  
- **Verification Issues:**
  - Ensure your documents are clear and meet the specified requirements.

> **Pro Tip:** Keep your banking information updated in BuildRail to avoid transaction delays.

## Conclusion

By following these steps, you can securely connect your business bank account to PayRail using Stripe Connect. This setup will facilitate faster and more secure financial transactions, allowing you to focus on your contracting work without financial hiccups.

Should you encounter issues, our support team is available to assist.
