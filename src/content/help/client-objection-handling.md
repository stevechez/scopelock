## Introduction

In the construction and contracting business, hearing "you're too expensive" from potential clients is common. Using BuildRail's Comm Vault scripts, you can professionally address this objection and reinforce your value. This guide will walk you through the steps to effectively use these scripts.

## Understanding Comm Vault Scripts

Before diving into the scripts, it's crucial to understand their purpose. Comm Vault scripts are pre-crafted templates designed to streamline communication and maintain professionalism. They help you address objections methodically, ensuring clear and consistent communication.

## Steps to Handle the "You're Too Expensive" Objection

### Step 1: Access the Comm Vault

1. **Log in** to your BuildRail account.
2. Navigate to the **Comm Vault** section from your dashboard.

### Step 2: Select the Appropriate Script

1. In the Comm Vault, locate the **Objections** section.
2. Find the script titled **"Addressing Cost Concerns"**.
3. Click on the script to view its contents.

### Step 3: Customize the Script

While Comm Vault scripts are ready-to-use, personalizing them can make your response more effective.

- **Insert Client Details**: Add the client's name and specific project details to personalize the communication.
- **Highlight Unique Value**: Modify sections to emphasize what sets your services apart. Focus on quality, reliability, and any unique offerings.

### Step 4: Send the Script

1. Use the **Send** feature within Comm Vault to email the script directly to your client.
2. Alternatively, copy the script text and paste it into your preferred communication platform.

### Step 5: Follow Up

- **Schedule a Follow-Up**: Set a reminder in BuildRail to follow up with the client within a few days.
- **Prepare for Questions**: Be ready to address any further concerns or queries the client may have.

## Best Practices

### Be Transparent

Whenever possible, provide clear breakdowns of costs to help clients understand where their money is going. Transparency builds trust.

### Offer Alternatives

If budget is a genuine concern, use the **ScopeLock** feature to offer alternative solutions or phased project approaches that align with the client's budget.

> **Pro Tip:** Use testimonials and past project success stories to reinforce your value. Real-world examples can be powerful tools in overcoming objections.

## Conclusion

Handling cost objections doesn't have to be daunting. With Comm Vault scripts, you can address "you're too expensive" objections professionally and effectively. Remember, the key is to communicate value, be transparent, and remain open to dialogue. By following these steps, you'll not only address the objection but also strengthen your client relationships.
