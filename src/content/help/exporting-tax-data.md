## Introduction

As a contractor using BuildRail's PayRail feature, it's essential to have a clear record of your financial transactions for accounting and tax purposes. This guide will walk you through exporting your year-end earnings and transaction fees from PayRail, ensuring your accountant has everything they need.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Accessing PayRail

1. **Log into BuildRail**: Start by logging into your BuildRail account with your username and password.
2. **Navigate to PayRail**: Once logged in, go to the dashboard and click on the **PayRail** section in the main menu.

### Exporting Earnings and Fees

#### Step 1: Select the Time Period

- **Choose 'Reports'**: In the PayRail section, click on **Reports**.
- **Set Date Range**: Select the 'Year-End' option or specify your custom date range for the year you wish to review.

#### Step 2: Generate the Report

- **Click 'Generate Report'**: After setting the date range, click the **Generate Report** button. This will compile all relevant data into a comprehensive report.
  
#### Step 3: Export the Data

- **Select Export Format**: Choose your preferred format for export, typically **CSV** or **PDF**.
- **Click 'Export'**: Click the **Export** button. Your file will automatically download to your device.

### Sending to Your Accountant

- **Locate the File**: Find the downloaded file in your device's download folder.
- **Email the File**: Attach the file to an email and send it directly to your accountant.

> **Pro Tip:** Keep a backup of your exported files in a secure location, such as a cloud storage service, for easy access in the future.

## Conclusion

Exporting your year-end earnings and transaction fees from PayRail is a straightforward process that ensures you and your accountant have all necessary financial records. Follow these steps to maintain organized and accurate financial documentation.

For further assistance, reach out to BuildRail support or consult your accountant for specific financial advice.
