## Introduction

Mistakenly dismissing a lead in Site Engine can happen to the best of us. Fortunately, BuildRail's Site Engine includes an archive feature that allows you to recover those leads quickly. This guide provides straightforward, step-by-step instructions for using the archive feature to retrieve a lead you’ve accidentally dismissed.

## Steps to Recover a Dismissed Lead

### Step 1: Access the Site Engine Dashboard

- **Log in** to your BuildRail account.
- Navigate to **Site Engine** from the main menu.

### Step 2: Open the Archive

- On the Site Engine dashboard, locate the **"Archive"** tab, usually found on the sidebar or under the "More" options.
- Click on **"Archive"** to open the list of archived leads.

### Step 3: Search for the Dismissed Lead

- Use the **search bar** at the top of the archive list to find the specific lead. You can search by lead name, project name, or any related keyword.
- Browse through the list if you prefer to locate the lead manually.

### Step 4: Recover the Lead

- Once you find the dismissed lead, click on it to view details.
- Look for the **"Restore"** button or option within the lead's details.
- Click **"Restore"** to move the lead back to your active leads list.

### Step 5: Verify Restoration

- Return to your main leads list in Site Engine.
- Check to ensure the lead is now back in the active list and accessible for further actions.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly check your archive to ensure no important leads are left behind. This can help maintain a streamlined workflow and prevent missed opportunities.

## Conclusion

The archive feature in Site Engine is a powerful tool for managing and recovering leads. By following these steps, you can quickly reinstate any leads you may have accidentally dismissed, ensuring you stay on top of all potential business opportunities.
