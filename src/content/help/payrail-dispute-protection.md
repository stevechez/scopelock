## Introduction

As a contractor, dealing with credit card chargebacks can be a frustrating and time-consuming process. BuildRail's **ScopeLock** feature is designed to protect your work and ensure you get paid for every change order. By having signed ScopeLock agreements, you can effectively defend against chargeback disputes and safeguard your revenue.

## Why ScopeLock Agreements Matter

Chargebacks often occur when clients dispute charges on their credit card. Without robust documentation, proving that the work was agreed upon and completed can be challenging. **ScopeLock agreements** provide a clear, documented trail of every change order, signed by both parties, which serves as compelling evidence in chargeback disputes.

## How to Use ScopeLock for Chargeback Disputes

### Step 1: Ensure Every Change Order is Documented

- **Create Change Orders:** When a change in the project scope occurs, immediately document it using ScopeLock.
- **Detail the Work:** Include specifics about the change, the cost, and the timeline. Be thorough and clear.

### Step 2: Obtain Client Signatures

- **Digital Signatures:** Use BuildRail to send the change order to your client for a digital signature.
- **Confirmation:** Ensure that you receive a signed copy back. This will serve as proof that the client agreed to the changes and associated costs.

### Step 3: Store Agreements Securely

- **Organize:** Keep all signed ScopeLock agreements organized within the BuildRail Comm Vault.
- **Access:** Ensure easy access to these documents should a dispute arise.

### Step 4: Respond to Chargeback Notices

- **Immediate Action:** Upon receiving a chargeback notice, promptly gather all relevant ScopeLock agreements.
- **Submit Evidence:** Provide the signed agreements as part of your dispute submission to the credit card company.

### Step 5: Follow Up

- **Communicate:** Stay in contact with the credit card company to ensure they have received all necessary documentation.
- **Monitor:** Track the status of your dispute until a resolution is reached.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly audit your ScopeLock agreements to ensure all documents are complete and signed. This proactive approach can save you time and enhance your dispute case.

## Conclusion

By leveraging BuildRail's signed ScopeLock agreements, you can significantly strengthen your position in credit card chargeback disputes. Remember, the key is in meticulous documentation and prompt action. Protect your work and your revenue by making ScopeLock an integral part of your project management process.
