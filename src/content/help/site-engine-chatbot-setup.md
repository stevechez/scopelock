## Introduction

In the competitive world of contracting, capturing leads around the clock can give you an edge. With BuildRail's AI Chatbot, you can engage potential clients even when you're not available. This guide will walk you through enabling the AI Chatbot on your website to ensure you never miss a lead, even at 2 AM.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access Your BuildRail Dashboard

1. **Log in** to your BuildRail account.
2. Navigate to the **Dashboard** where you manage your site features.

### Step 2: Navigate to Comm Vault

1. In the main menu, select **Comm Vault**. This module manages your communications and is where you can set up the chatbot.
2. Click on the **AI Chatbot** tab to access chatbot settings.

### Step 3: Configure the AI Chatbot

1. **Enable the Chatbot**:
   - Find the toggle switch labeled **"Enable AI Chatbot"** and turn it on.
   
2. **Set Chatbot Availability**:
   - Under the availability settings, ensure the option for **24/7 Availability** is checked.
   - This ensures the chatbot can engage visitors at any time, including 2 AM.

### Step 4: Customize Chatbot Interactions

1. **Create Lead Capture Prompts**:
   - Use the **Prompt Builder** to set up questions that capture essential lead information, such as name, contact details, and project needs.
   
2. **Set Response Templates**:
   - Customize automated responses to common inquiries to keep potential leads engaged.

> **Pro Tip:** Tailor your prompts to reflect your brand's voice and the specific services you offer to increase engagement.

### Step 5: Deploy the Chatbot on Your Website

1. **Copy the Embed Code**:
   - Once configured, click **Generate Embed Code**. 
   - Copy the code snippet provided.

2. **Insert the Code into Your Website**:
   - Access your website's HTML editor.
   - Paste the code snippet just before the closing `</body>` tag on your website pages where you want the chatbot to appear.

### Step 6: Test the Chatbot

1. **Visit Your Website**:
   - Open your website in a browser to ensure the chatbot appears and functions as expected.
   
2. **Simulate a Lead Interaction**:
   - At 2 AM, or during off-hours, simulate an inquiry to confirm the chatbot captures lead information correctly.

## Conclusion

By following these steps, your AI Chatbot will be ready to capture leads even while you sleep. This powerful tool ensures you're always available to potential clients, increasing your chances of winning new business. Remember to regularly review and update your chatbot's scripts to keep the interaction relevant and engaging.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly check the chatbot's lead capture logs in Comm Vault to follow up promptly with new prospects.
