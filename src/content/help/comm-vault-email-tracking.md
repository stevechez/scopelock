## Introduction

Understanding client engagement with your proposals is crucial for strategic follow-ups and improving your bid success rate. BuildRail's Site Engine feature allows you to track exactly when and how many times a client opens your proposal. This guide will walk you through the process.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Accessing Your Proposal

1. **Log in to BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access the BuildRail dashboard.
2. **Navigate to Site Engine**: From the main menu, select **Site Engine** to access your proposals.

### Viewing Proposal Engagement

1. **Select the Proposal**: In the Site Engine, find and click on the specific proposal you want to track.
2. **View Engagement Details**:
   - On the proposal details page, locate the **Engagement** section.
   - Here, you'll see a summary of client interactions, including:
     - **First Opened**: The exact date and time the client first opened the proposal.
     - **Total Views**: The total number of times the client has viewed the proposal.

### Analyzing Engagement Data

- **First Opened**: Use this timestamp to determine when to follow up with the client.
- **Total Views**: A high number of views can indicate strong interest; consider reaching out to address any questions or concerns they may have.

> **Pro Tip**: If a proposal is frequently viewed but not acted upon, it may be beneficial to contact the client to offer assistance or clarification.

## Conclusion

By leveraging BuildRail's Site Engine, you can gain valuable insights into client behavior and tailor your follow-up strategies accordingly. Stay informed and responsive to maximize your chances of closing deals.
