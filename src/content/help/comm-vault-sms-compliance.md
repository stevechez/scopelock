## Introduction

As a contractor using BuildRail, ensuring your business texts reach clients without being blocked by mobile carriers is crucial. The A2P 10DLC (Application-to-Person 10-Digit Long Code) system is designed to enhance the deliverability and reliability of business messaging. This guide will walk you through the registration process to keep your communications flowing smoothly.

## What is A2P 10DLC?

A2P 10DLC is a messaging system that allows businesses to send messages via 10-digit long codes with improved throughput and delivery rates. It is tailored for business-to-customer communication, ensuring that your messages are not mistaken for spam.

## Why Register?

- **Compliance:** Comply with carrier regulations to avoid message blocking.
- **Deliverability:** Increase the likelihood of your messages reaching clients.
- **Reputation:** Establish your business as a legitimate sender.

## Step-by-Step Registration Process

### Step 1: Gather Required Information

Before starting the registration, ensure you have the following information ready:

- **Business Name:** The official name of your contracting business.
- **Business Type:** Specify if you are a sole proprietor, LLC, corporation, etc.
- **Tax Identification Number (TIN):** Your business's TIN or EIN.
- **Contact Information:** Primary contact's name, email, and phone number.
- **Use Case Description:** A brief explanation of how you use messaging in your business (e.g., appointment reminders, job status updates).

### Step 2: Choose a Messaging Provider

Select a messaging provider that supports A2P 10DLC. Many providers offer this service, so choose one that integrates well with BuildRail and your existing systems.

### Step 3: Submit Registration

Once you've chosen a provider:

1. **Access the Provider's Portal:** Log into your messaging provider's platform.
2. **Locate A2P 10DLC Registration:** Navigate to the registration section usually found under "Settings" or "Compliance."
3. **Input Your Information:** Fill out the required fields with the information gathered in Step 1.
4. **Submit for Approval:** After completing the form, submit your application for carrier approval.

### Step 4: Await Confirmation

- **Approval Time:** Carrier approval typically takes a few days. Ensure your contact information is accurate for any follow-up questions.
- **Confirmation:** Once approved, you will receive a confirmation email from your provider.

### Step 5: Start Messaging

After approval:

- **Test Your Messaging:** Send test messages to ensure proper delivery.
- **Monitor Performance:** Use your provider's analytics to track message performance and troubleshoot any issues.

> **Pro Tip:** Regularly review your messaging practices to maintain compliance and optimize message deliverability.

## Conclusion

Registering for A2P 10DLC is essential for contractors using BuildRail to ensure their business texts reach clients reliably. By following this guide, you can navigate the registration process smoothly, enhancing your communication efficiency and effectiveness.

For further assistance, contact your messaging provider or the BuildRail support team.
