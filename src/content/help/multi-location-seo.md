## Introduction
If your crew operates across county lines, it's essential to have dedicated landing pages for each city. This guide walks you through setting up separate landing pages in BuildRail to improve local visibility and win more bids.

## Benefits of City-Specific Landing Pages
- **Increased Visibility**: Improve search engine rankings in each city.
- **Targeted Messaging**: Tailor your services and messaging to the specific needs of each city.
- **Higher Conversion Rates**: Speak directly to local customers, increasing your chances of winning bids.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Access Site Engine

1. **Log in to BuildRail**: Use your credentials to access your BuildRail dashboard.
2. **Navigate to Site Engine**: From the main menu, click on "Site Engine."

### 2. Create New Landing Page

1. **Select 'Create New Page'**: Click on "Create New Page" to start a new landing page.
2. **Choose a Template**: Select a template that matches your brand and is suitable for a city-specific page.

### 3. Customize Page Content

1. **City-Specific Information**: 
   - **Title**: Use a clear, city-specific title (e.g., "Top Contractors in [City Name]").
   - **Services Offered**: List services highlighting any city-specific specialties or regulations.
   - **Testimonials**: Include reviews from clients in the city if available.

2. **SEO Optimization**: 
   - **Keywords**: Use city-specific keywords throughout your content.
   - **Meta Description**: Write a concise meta description with the city name.

### 4. Add City-Specific Images

1. **Upload Relevant Images**: Use images that reflect the city or past projects completed there.
2. **Optimize Images**: Ensure images are optimized for fast loading speeds.

### 5. Configure Contact Information

1. **Local Contact Details**: Provide a local phone number or address if applicable.
2. **Contact Form**: Include a form for potential clients to request a quote or consultation.

### 6. Publish and Test

1. **Preview the Page**: Review the page for errors and ensure all links work.
2. **Publish**: Once satisfied, publish the page.
3. **Test**: Check the page on various devices to ensure it looks good and functions properly.

> **Pro Tip**: Regularly update the landing pages with new projects, testimonials, and any changes in services to keep the content fresh and relevant.

## Conclusion
By following these steps, you can effectively set up dedicated landing pages for each city you service, increasing your reach and potential for acquiring new business. Remember, a well-optimized landing page is a powerful tool in the competitive contracting market.
