## Introduction
In the fast-paced world of contracting, following up on proposals is crucial. Clients who ghost your proposals can stall your projects and affect your cash flow. BuildRail's automated SMS and email sequences can help you re-engage these clients effortlessly. Follow these steps to set up your sequences effectively.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Access Comm Vault
1. **Log in** to your BuildRail account.
2. Navigate to **Comm Vault** in the main dashboard.

### Step 2: Create a New Sequence
1. Click on **Sequences** in the left-hand menu.
2. Select **Create New Sequence**.

### Step 3: Define Your Audience
1. Choose **"Clients Who Haven't Responded"** as your target audience.
2. Use filters to refine your list based on criteria such as proposal date, project type, or client location.

### Step 4: Set Up SMS and Email Templates
1. **Select SMS** or **Email** for your first message.
2. Click on **Create New Template**.
3. Draft your message:
   - **Email**: Include a polite reminder and a summary of the proposal.
   - **SMS**: Keep it short and include a direct question to elicit a response.
4. Save your template.

### Step 5: Schedule the Sequence
1. Choose the time interval for each follow-up (e.g., 3 days after the initial proposal).
2. Decide on the number of follow-ups (recommended: 3 total).
3. Set specific times for sending, keeping in mind your client's local time zone.

### Step 6: Activate the Sequence
1. Review your sequence settings.
2. Click **Activate** to start sending.

## Monitoring and Adjusting

### Review Sequence Performance
- Navigate to **Comm Vault** > **Sequences** > **Performance** tab.
- Analyze open rates, response rates, and engagement stats.

### Modify as Needed
- Use insights to tweak timing, messaging, or frequency.
- Reactivate the sequence after making changes.

> **Pro Tip:** Personalize your messages where possible. Use the client's name or refer to specific project details to increase engagement.

## Conclusion
Setting up automated SMS and email sequences in BuildRail helps ensure that no opportunity is lost due to lack of follow-up. By following the steps outlined, you can maintain a steady flow of communication with your clients and improve your proposal acceptance rates.
